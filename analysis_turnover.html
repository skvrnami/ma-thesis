April 10, 2016

Some charts are here. It seems that established political parties rarely nominate a candidate who run on a minor party’s list in the previous election, probably because they have enough of their candidates (and it would cause internal clashes…). New parties (TOP09, VV, ANO etc.) sometimes exploit candidates from minor parties. Also, there is a flux of candidates between minor parties.
